Arithmetic with numbers¶

Add, subtract, multiply and divide¶

+ plus adds numbers.

1+2
3

- minus subtracts numbers.

1-2
-1

* asterisk multiplies numbers.

5*2
10

Warning

Juxtaposition does not work for multiplication. The code 5(2) will reult in the following error.

Error in eval(expr, envir, enclos): attempt to apply non-function

/ forward slash divides numbers.

4/2
2

Parentheses and exponents¶

() parentheses group computations.

(1+2)*5
15

^ caret and ** two asterisks both raise numbers two powers.

5^2
25
5**2
25

Style Tip

You can decide which method of inputting exponents you prefer, but for the sake of clarity you should only use one method in a given notebook.

Note

R uses the standard order of operations when evaluating arithmetic expressions: parentheses, exponents, multiplication, division, addition, subtraction.

Example: Order of operations¶

\[1 + 4 \cdot 3 ^ 2 - \frac{5}{2} = 34.5\]
1 + 4 * 3 ^ 2 - 5 / 2
34.5
\[\frac{1 + (4 \cdot 3) ^ 2 - 10}{2}=70\]
(1+(4*3)^2-5)/2
70
